Ordering

You can browse our collection, add items to your cart, and click "Buy All on WhatsApp" to securely place your order directly with our sales team.

Orders can only be modified within 12 hours of placing them via WhatsApp before they enter production or dispatch.

We accept UPI, Bank Transfers, and major Credit/Debit cards. Payment links are shared securely via WhatsApp during checkout.

Shipping & Delivery

Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days for ready-made items. Custom orders require an additional 3-5 days for production.

Once dispatched, we will send an exclusive tracking link and AWB number to your WhatsApp number.

Custom Jerseys

Check out our Custom Design page for a full step-by-step breakdown of how we turn your vision into reality.

We support both team orders and individual requests. The MOQ for full sublimated team wear is usually 5 pieces, but single prints are possible for pre-made styles.

Product Care

Machine wash cold with like colors inside out. Do not use bleach or fabric softeners. Tumble dry on low heat or hang dry for best results.

Never iron directly on the printed designs. Iron inside out on minimum heat if necessary, though our premium fabrics rarely wrinkle.

Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about our logistics and policies.

Shipping Policy

Delivery Timeframes

We leverage top-tier logistics partners to ensure your products arrive safe and fast.

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days from dispatch.
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days from dispatch (available in select metro cities).
  • Custom Orders: Require an additional 3-5 days for production before dispatch.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated automatically at checkout based on your location and the weight of your order. We offer free standard shipping on orders above ₹3,000.

Tracking Information

Once your order is processed and dispatched, you will receive an AWB tracking number via WhatsApp and email. You can use this to track the live status of your package.

Returns Policy

Return Window

We offer a hassle-free 7-day return window starting from the day your package is delivered.

Conditions for Returns

For a return to be eligible, the item must be:

  • Unused, unwashed, and in the exact condition it was received.
  • Possess all original tags intimately attached.
  • Packed securely in its original packaging.

Non-Returnable Items

Please note that all custom-designed jerseys are printed uniquely for you, and therefore, cannot be returned or exchanged unless there is a manufacturing defect.

Return Process

To initiate a return, contact our support team on WhatsApp at +91 73586 41670 with your order details and reason. Our courier partner will pick up the item within 48 hours of approval.

Refund Timeline

Once the returned item passes our quality check, your refund will be processed to the original mode of payment within 5-7 business days.

Exchange Policy

Size Exchanges

If the jersey doesn't fit right, we offer a one-time free size exchange for non-custom items. The exchange window is 7 days from delivery. Simply message us on WhatsApp with your requested size, and we will handle the pickup and replacement drop-off.

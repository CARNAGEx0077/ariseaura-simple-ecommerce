How to Measure

  • 1. Chest

    Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the measuring tape horizontal.

  • 2. Length

    Measure from the highest point of the shoulder down to the desired hemline.

  • 3. Shoulder

    Measure straight across the back from the edge of one shoulder to the other.

Pro Fit Tips

Slim Fit vs Regular Fit

Our authentic game jerseys naturally feature a slimmer, more athletic cut to prevent drag. For a more relaxed streetwear fit, we recommend sizing up one size.

Between Sizes?

If your measurements fall between two sizes, choose the smaller size for a tighter fit or the larger size for a looser fit.
